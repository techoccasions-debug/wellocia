Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted HR partner — recognized for transforming the way businesses attract, develop, and retain talent. We envision a future where every organization has access to the right talent, and every professional finds meaningful work aligned with their potential.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, people-centric HR solutions that create lasting value for our clients and candidates. Through ethical practices, deep industry knowledge, and a commitment to excellence, we bridge the gap between exceptional talent and exceptional opportunity.

Leadership

Director's Message

Mr. Raisingh Patel
Founder & Director
"

When we founded Wellocia in 2007, our goal was simple: to build a recruitment firm that people could genuinely trust — one that puts relationships above transactions, and long-term value above quick placements.

Over seventeen years, we have placed hundreds of professionals across sectors as diverse as Power, Oil & Gas, IT, and Manufacturing. Every placement represents a family supported, a career advanced, and a business strengthened. That responsibility is never lost on us.

As India's workforce evolves, so do we. We are continuously investing in our team, our processes, and our understanding of the industries we serve — so that our clients and candidates always receive guidance they can count on.
